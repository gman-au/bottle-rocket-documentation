# Page symbols

## Rocketbook
From the [Rocketbook site](https://getrocketbook.com.au/pages/learn-more):

> The seven symbols at the bottom of each page are the magic “buttons” behind the Rocketbook’s quick and easy cloud organization. You assign each icon to the destination of your choice, any folder inside Dropbox, Evernote, Google Docs, iCloud, Box, Slack, OneNote, or your email address. When you mark that icon on your page, the Rocketbook app automatically files your notes in the right place.

<div class="text--center">
![Rocketbook page symbols](/img/rocketbookPageSymbols.png)
</div>

For the purposes of the Bottle Rocket documentation, the seven symbols 
above are referred to by the following name(s), in order:

* Rocket
* Diamond
* Apple
* Bell
* Clover
* Star
* Horseshoe
